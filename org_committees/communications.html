Communications

The Communications team plays an indispensable role in the making of DHAP Writes! 2020. They are your first and last line of contact; they are the ones extending invitation and information to you. From the very first registration to subsequent updates, the communications sub-committee is by your side every step of the way.

They warmly welcome you to participate in DHAP Writes! 2020!
