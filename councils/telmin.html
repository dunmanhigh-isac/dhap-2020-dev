ASEAN Telecommunications and IT Meeting

The First ASEAN Telecommunications Ministers Meeting (TELMIN) was held on 13-14 July 2001 in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. The ASEAN ICT Masterplan 2015 marked 10 years of ICT cooperation within AMS. In today’s day and age of digital connectivity, participants will be looking to craft measures suited to bring ASEAN closer to its vision of a global ICT hub.

The Question of Information and Communication Technology in Education

While the ASEAN Member States are increasingly cognisant of the need to develop their education systems with technology, much still needs to be done to integrate ICT into ASEAN education to aid students directly in their learning. The integration of ICT into education can also help AMS indirectly by enhancing the abilities of their future workforce. Participants are expected to incorporate the technicalities of ICT into the familiar topic of education, by proposing solutions that resolve typical issues regarding education in ASEAN with the support of ICT infrastructure in specific. They are also reminded to be sensitive towards the discrepancy between the ICT capabilities of AMS.
