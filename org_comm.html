Administrative Board

The Administrative Board forms the backbone of DHAP Writes! 2020. Every one of our four sub-committees work towards the same goal to ensure DHAP Writes! 2020 is a great experience for all participants. From their publicity outreach to the aesthetics you will enjoy, the administrative board is made up of diligent ISACers who hope to make your competition experience an enriching and smooth sailing one. The Publicity and Communications sub-committee are your first line of contact when it comes to DHAP Writes! 2020, while our Design sub-committee pleases your aesthetic taste buds. Last but not least, the Allocations sub-committee ensures that your preferred council becomes a reality!
